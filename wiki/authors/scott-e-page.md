# Scott E. Page

Complexity scientist and author of "Diversity and Complexity" and the audiocourse "The Hidden Factor: Why Thinking Differently Is Your Greatest Asset." His work provides the theoretical foundation for understanding the relationship between diversity and complexity, and his framing of individual agency in complex systems is a recurring reference in the blog.

## Key contributions
- Key quote: "An actor in a complex system controls almost nothing, but influences almost everything" -- captures the distinction between control and influence that is central to addressing complex problems
- Provides rigorous frameworks for understanding how diversity drives exploration, innovation, and resilience in complex systems, and how its reduction leads to fragility and groupthink
- Recommended by the blog author as a primary reference for deeper understanding of diversity mechanisms, coupling effects, and collective problem-solving in complex systems
