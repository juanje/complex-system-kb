# Todd Hargrove

Movement and pain science practitioner, author of "A Guide to Better Movement." His key contribution to this knowledge base is framing expert disagreement as a diagnostic signal for complexity rather than a failure of knowledge -- a heuristic that the blog author uses as a foundational lens for understanding health.

## Key contributions
- Key quote: "When qualified experts have significant disagreements about the basics of how to solve a problem, it is most likely complex" -- provides a practical test for identifying complex problems
- Bridges movement science and complexity thinking, connecting biomechanics, pain science, and rehabilitation to complex systems principles
- Key intellectual influence on Juanje Ojeda's approach to health and movement, with his work cited as both opening epigraph and conceptual foundation across multiple articles
