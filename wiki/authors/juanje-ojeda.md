# Juanje Ojeda

Health and movement practitioner who applies complexity thinking to health, mobility, chronic pain, and skill development. Author of "3 Pasos Contra el Sedentarismo" (2019), which includes a full chapter on complex systems applied to health, and creator of a Spanish-language blog series that bridges academic complexity science with practical, accessible understanding.

## Key contributions
- Years of experience applying complex systems concepts with clients working on mobility, health, and chronic pain, demonstrating that complexity ideas are intuitive and useful in practice
- Published a blog series systematically connecting complex systems properties (emergence, non-linearity, attractors, feedback, diversity) to health, movement, and skill development
- Advocates for treating health as a complex problem requiring adaptive, context-dependent approaches rather than one-size-fits-all prescriptions
